# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

OpenFrame and its multi-platform ecosystem ("we," "our," or "us") respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services across all platforms including OpenMSP, Flamingo, Admin Hub, OpenFrame, and related applications.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, organization details, and authentication credentials
- **Profile Data**: User preferences, settings, and customization choices
- **Communication Data**: Messages, support tickets, and feedback you provide

### Technical Information
- **Usage Analytics**: How you interact with our services, features used, and performance metrics
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers
- **System Telemetry**: Application performance data, error logs, and diagnostic information
- **Integration Data**: Credentials and configuration for third-party services (stored encrypted)

### Collected Automatically
- **Log Files**: Server logs, access times, and system events
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session management and user experience optimization
- **Performance Metrics**: Application responsiveness and reliability data

## How We Use Your Information

### Service Provision
- To provide, maintain, and improve our multi-platform services
- To authenticate users and secure accounts
- To enable integrations with MSP tools and third-party services
- To process transactions and manage subscriptions

### Communication
- To send important service updates and security notifications
- To respond to support requests and provide customer service
- To send newsletters and product updates (with consent)

### Analytics and Improvement
- To analyze usage patterns and optimize platform performance
- To develop new features and enhance existing functionality
- To conduct research and analytics for business insights

### Legal and Security
- To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
- To enforce our terms of service and community guidelines

## Data Security and Protection

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3
- **Access Controls**: Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
- **Infrastructure Security**: Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
- **Monitoring**: Real-time threat detection and incident response procedures
- **Regular Audits**: Quarterly security assessments and penetration testing

### Data Storage
- **Primary Storage**: Secure cloud databases with automatic backups
- **Backup Systems**: Encrypted, geographically distributed backup storage
- **Retention Policies**: Data retained only as long as necessary for service provision
- **Geographic Location**: Data processed and stored in compliance with applicable laws

### Data Sharing
We may share data with:

- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request copies of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
- **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we process your data

### Communication Preferences
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- **Preference Center**: Manage notification settings
- **Consent Withdrawal**: Revoke previously given consent

### Data Subject Rights (GDPR)
For EU residents, additional rights include:
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### Cookie Usage
We use cookies and similar technologies for:
- **Authentication**: Secure login and session management
- **Preferences**: Remembering user settings and customizations
- **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns and platform performance
- **Security**: Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access

### Cookie Management
You can control cookies through:
- Browser settings to block or delete cookies
- Our cookie preference center
- Third-party opt-out mechanisms

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.
